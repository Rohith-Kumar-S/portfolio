About Me

My interest is in diagnosing where things go wrong and optimizing for performance, whether that’s a flawed assumption in an ML architecture or a bottleneck in an LLM orchestration pipeline.

Currently a Master's in Data Science student at Northeastern University, I focus on deep learning, computer vision, and LLM systems. I have cross-functional domain expertise in Autonomous Driving, E-Commerce Search & Discovery, and Healthcare Life Sciences, with recent work spanning multimodal motion prediction, robotic manipulation, recommendation systems optimization, Agentic AI, and medical image segmentation. With 3 years of experience building full-stack backend and enterprise solutions, I am well-versed in developing product-specific tools using Core Java and Spring Boot and deploying containerized applications across AWS and Docker.
